Safety Induction Booking

In order to use Workshop7, You first require to be safety inducted. This in addition to terms and conditions protects you and The University of Canberra incase accidents happen. You will be shown how to use commonly used machines and shown all required safety points. Without completing induction, bookings will be denied, if you have already been inducted before this system this is not required.
